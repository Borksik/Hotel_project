About

When Fontainebleau was created, movies and television defined entertainment. Today, entertainment is experiencing a revolution. Observation has become participation. The timeless Fontainebleau brand has emerged from the original vision of legendary architect Morris Lapidus whose daring designs epitomized a concept life "more unusual, interesting, warm". Today, Fontainebleau Miami Beach weaves its brand making attributes - striking design, contemporary art, music, fashion and technology - into vibrant new kind of immersive guest experience that will launch a generation preferred international resorts.
